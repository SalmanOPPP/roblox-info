Roblox Doors

Doors is a popular horror game on Roblox.

Players navigate through a series of increasingly terrifying rooms while being hunted by a monstrous entity.

Key Features

Intense Atmosphere: Jump scares, eerie sounds, and a chilling ambiance create a truly frightening experience.
Challenging Puzzles: Solve puzzles to progress through the levels and avoid the entity.
Multiple Endings: Discover different outcomes based on your choices and actions.
Regular Updates: The developers frequently add new content, keeping the game fresh and exciting.

Tips for Survival

Stay Quiet: Make as little noise as possible to avoid attracting the entity.
Use the Lights Wisely: Illuminate dark areas to see potential threats, but be mindful of the entity's attraction to light.
Find Hiding Spots: Utilize closets, lockers, and other hiding places to escape the entity's pursuit.
Work Together: If playing with friends, communicate and coordinate your actions to increase your chances of survival.

Disclaimer: Doors is a horror game and may not be suitable for all players. Viewer discretion is advised.
